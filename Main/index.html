About us

Driving Growth Through Quality & Innovation

CSB GLOBAL TRADING FZCO, established in 2024 in Dubai, is a growing general trading and facility management company serving the UAE and global markets. It operates across multiple industries, including minerals, M.E.P. services, manufacturing, hospitality, and safety products. Committed to quality and innovation, the company aims to expand its presence in Africa, the Middle East, and Asia.

  • Global Trade Network - Linking suppliers and customers for efficient trade.
  • Quality & Competitive Pricing - Providing top-tier services at the best market rates.
  • Seamless Transactions - Simplifying processes for ease and trust.
  • Sustainable Work Culture - Ensuring long-term efficiency and excellence.
At CSB GLOBAL TRADING, we uphold the highest standards through hard work, honesty, and commitment, ensuring top-quality service for our clients. - Director's Message
